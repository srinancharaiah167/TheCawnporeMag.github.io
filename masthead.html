Kritika Singh (She/her)
Founder and Editor-in-Chief

Kritika, originating from India, is a versatile individual proficient in writing, art, programming, and editing. Her ardor for articulating creativity through storytelling has culminated in her works being featured in reputable platforms such as Teen Ink, Ritambhara (her university magazine), Alfaz-tere-mere and The Library of Ink. Throughout her academic pursuit, she garnered numerous accolades for her adeptness in creative writing in both English and Hindi, her mother tongue, which served as a substantial source of impetus. In her leisure hours, Kritika immerses herself in literary pursuits, delving into books and indulging in Netflix series. She's currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Physics while also concurrently holding the position of an editor at a few magazines.
